Want to play a game with your friends?

During the epidemic, did you have fewer opportunities to go out with your friends to have fun? Have you become distant because of this? Then let's play a game of tacit understanding. Test how well you know your friends, and how well they know you!

Here're the rules!

  1. Response to six random questions from our question pool. Remember to reveal your true self!
  2. Receive your form id that your friends can later track on.
  3. send the link to your friend and let them guess your answers.
  4. Let your friends tell you how well they did on this game.
  5. ps.If one of your friend get a relative low score, you really should get a chance to hang out together (at a safe place)!
